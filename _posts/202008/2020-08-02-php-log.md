```PHP
$log_txt = $userid."|".$datetime."|".$data."|".$ip."|".$idx;
$log_dir = "/home/www/data/log"; 
$log_file = fopen($log_dir."/log.txt", "a");
fwrite($log_file, $log_txt."\r\n");
fclose($log_file);
```
